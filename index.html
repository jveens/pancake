pancake

A deliciously simple CSS grid. Enjoy it however you want* with as many stacks as you like. Put them in a griddle and go.*Maple syrup optional

Usage

Pancake is quick to configure and easy to use. Define your grid in config.scss and compile away. It’s up to you whether you want to use presentational classes in your HTML, or use the grid mixins right in your Sass files.

$class-prefix  : 'pancake';
$stack-number  : 12;
$space-between : 15px;
@include stack-span(1)
